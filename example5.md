EN: ## **Act Breakdown**  
ZH: ## **幕次概要**  

EN: ### **Act 1: Setup**  
ZH: ### **第一幕：铺垫**  

EN: *Establishes the Lighthouse Festival, introduces the characters, and plants the seeds of conflict.*  
ZH: *确立灯塔节背景，引入角色，并埋下冲突的种子。*  

EN: - **Key Goals**: Introduce Lila’s restlessness (wanting to leave), Jasper’s return (confronting his past), and Eleanor’s torn feelings (between Tom and Jasper).  
ZH: - **核心目标**：展现莉拉的不安（渴望离开）、贾斯珀的归来（直面过去）、埃莉诺的矛盾（在汤姆与贾斯珀间挣扎）。  

EN: - **Symbolic Beat**: The lighthouse—introduced as a symbol of roots and unresolved pasts.  
ZH: - **象征节点**：灯塔——作为根基与未愈过往的象征被引入。  


EN: ### **Act 2: Confrontation**  
ZH: ### **第二幕：对峙**  

EN: *Escalates the conflicts: Eleanor admits her feelings for Jasper, Mabel confronts Lila about leaving.*  
ZH: *冲突升级：埃莉诺承认对贾斯珀的感情，梅布尔就离开一事与莉拉对峙。*  

EN: - **Key Goals**: Force characters to face their fears (Mabel’s fear of losing Lila, Eleanor’s fear of choosing passion over safety).  
ZH: - **核心目标**：迫使角色直面恐惧（梅布尔害怕失去莉拉，埃莉诺害怕选择激情而非安稳）。  

EN: - **Symbolic Beat**: The train ticket (Lila’s desire to escape) and the engagement ring (Eleanor’s trapped state).  
ZH: - **象征节点**：火车票（莉拉逃离的渴望）与订婚戒指（埃莉诺被困的状态）。  


EN: ### **Act 3: Resolution**  
ZH: ### **第三幕：和解**  

EN: *Characters resolve their conflicts: Eleanor chooses Jasper, Lila chooses to stay, and Mabel finds peace with her lost dreams.*  
ZH: *角色化解冲突：埃莉诺选择贾斯珀，莉拉选择留下，梅布尔与消逝的梦想和解。*  

EN: - **Key Goals**: Tie themes of tradition, dreams, and love to the festival.  
ZH: - **核心目标**：将传统、梦想与爱的主题与灯塔节联结。  

EN: - **Symbolic Beat**: Lanterns—released as a symbol of hope and new beginnings.  
ZH: - **象征节点**：灯笼——被放飞，象征希望与新生。

ZH: **动作**：贾斯珀将骨灰撒向风里，读信中的一句（*“爸，对不起，我没早点回来”*），然后把信放进罐中。埃莉诺起初没看见他——随后倒吸一口气。  

EN: **Dialogue**:  
ZH: **对话**：

ZH: **动作**：梅布尔将行李箱重重摔在桌上。莉拉吓了一跳，一脸心虚。

It’s the day you took your first steps, the day you painted your first lighthouse, the day you told me you wanted to be an artist. If you leave, you’re not just leaving the town. You’re leaving me." (Lila sits down, taking Mabel’s hand.)

ZH: **潜台词**：梅布尔的愤怒掩盖了失去莉拉的恐惧。莉拉的爆发暴露了她因离开而产生的愧疚，以及被困住的挫败感。杂烩汤（家族传统）象征她们的羁绊。  

EN: **Act 2 Curtain**: Lila hugs Mabel, whispering *"I’m sorry,"* as Mabel cries into her shoulder. The train ticket falls out of the suitcase and lies on the floor.  
ZH: **第二幕落幕**：莉拉抱住梅布尔，轻声说"对不起"，梅布尔哭着埋进她的肩窝。火车票从行李箱里滑出，落在地上。

ZH: **动作**：梅布尔拿起话筒。

When I was young, I wanted to be a painter. I wanted to travel, to see the world. But then I had Lila, and my dream changed. My new dream was to raise her, to give her a home, to watch her grow into the amazing woman she is today." (Lila tears up, looking at Mabel.) "Lila, if you’re listening: dreams don’t have to die. They just change shape. And if your dream is to be an artist, I’ll be right there with you—even if that means you have to leave."

- **莉拉**（走上舞台）："我不想离开了。"（她撕碎火车票。）"我想留下。我想画节日、画灯塔、画你。我想在这儿当艺术家，和你一起。"  
- **梅布尔**（抱住她）："哦，莉拉。这是最棒的梦想。"（人群欢呼。贾斯珀和埃莉诺牵手走来，克拉拉和汤姆微笑着加入。）

EN: **Subtext**: Mabel’s speech ties together the play’s themes (tradition, dreams, love). Lila tearing up the train ticket symbolizes her choice to blend her dream of art with her home.  
ZH: **潜台词**：梅布尔的发言串联起全剧主题（传统、梦想、爱）。莉拉撕碎火车票，象征她选择将艺术梦想与家乡融合。  

EN: **Act 3 Curtain**: The camera lingers on Lila’s sketchbook—showing a drawing of the town square, Mabel hugging her, and Jasper and Eleanor holding hands. The sound of the crowd cheering and ocean waves fills the air.  
ZH: **第三幕落幕**：镜头停留在莉拉的素描本上——画中是广场、拥抱的梅布尔，以及牵手的贾斯珀和埃莉诺。人群的欢呼声与海浪声回荡在空中。

1. **Tradition vs. Change**: The Lighthouse Festival symbolizes tradition, but the characters learn to blend tradition with growth (e.g., Lila staying to paint the town).

EN: 2. **Dreams and Sacrifice**: Mabel’s sacrifice (giving up painting for Lila) teaches Lila that dreams can evolve, not die.  
ZH: 2. **梦想与牺牲**：梅布尔的牺牲（为了莉拉放弃绘画）教会了莉拉，梦想可以演变，而非消亡。  

EN: 3. **Home and Belonging**: Home isn’t just a place—it’s the people you love. Lila and Jasper find belonging in the town and their relationships.  
ZH: 3. **家与归属感**：家不只是一个地方——而是你爱的人。莉拉和贾斯珀在小镇和彼此的关系中找到了归属感。
