EN: ## **Act 1: Setup**  
ZH: ## **第一幕：铺垫**  

EN: * **Time**: 2:00 AM, desert night.  
ZH: * **时间**：凌晨2点，沙漠夜。  

EN: * **Setting**: Outpost Rimon 20—an isolated Israeli defensive position in the Golan Heights. Sandbags, barbed wire, a rusted machine gun (MG) nest, and a medic’s tent dot the perimeter. Syrian tanks rumble in the distance, faint but growing louder.  
ZH: * **场景**：里蒙20号前哨站——戈兰高地上一个孤立的以色列防御据点。沙袋墙、铁丝网、生锈的机枪（MG）工事、医护帐篷散布四周。叙利亚坦克在远处轰鸣，声音微弱但越来越响。  


EN: ### **Scene 1: Perimeter Defense**  
ZH: ### **场景1：外围防御**  

EN: * **Action**: Lt. Dan crouches behind a sandbag wall, counting ammo boxes (50 rounds per soldier). Pvt. Moshe struggles to fix a bent MG barrel, his hands shaking.  
ZH: * **动作**：丹中尉蹲在沙袋墙后清点弹药箱（每名士兵50发）。摩西列兵颤抖着试图修好弯曲的机枪枪管。  

EN: * **Dialogue Notes**:  
ZH: * **对话提示**：  

EN:   - **Dan**: (gruff, focused) “Moshe, that MG needs to fire by dawn. We can’t stop a T-55 with a pistol.”  
ZH:   - **丹**：（粗哑，专注）“摩西，这挺机枪必须在黎明前修好。用手枪挡不住T-55坦克。”  

EN:   - **Moshe**: (nervous, dropping a tool) “I’m trying, sir! The last shell bent the barrel like a paper clip!”  
ZH:   - **摩西**：（紧张，掉落工具）“我在努力，长官！上一发炮弹把枪管砸得像回形针！”  

EN:   - **Dan**: (softening) “Breathe. You fixed the generator last week—you can do this.”  
ZH:   - **丹**：（语气缓和）“深呼吸。上周你修好了发电机——这次也能行。”  

EN: * **Subtext**: Dan is trying to stay in control, but his jaw tightens every time the tanks rumble. Moshe is terrified of failing his brother’s legacy.  
ZH: * **潜台词**：丹努力保持冷静，但每当坦克轰鸣时，他的下巴就会紧绷。摩西害怕辜负哥哥的遗志。  


EN: ### **Scene 2: Medic’s Tent**  
ZH: ### **场景2：医护帐篷**  

EN: * **Action**: Medic Leah cleans a shrapnel wound on Pvt. David’s arm. A canteen sits on a crate—half-empty.  
ZH: * **动作**：莉娅医护兵为大卫列兵清理手臂的弹片伤。板条箱上放着一个半满的水壶。  

EN: * **Dialogue Notes**:  
ZH: * **对话提示**：  

EN:   - **Leah**: (calm, but urgent) “David, this is a scratch. Save the water—one canteen per person for 12 hours. That’s non-negotiable.”  
ZH:   - **莉娅**：（冷静但急切）“大卫，只是擦伤。省着点水——每人12小时配给一壶。没得商量。”  

EN:   - **David**: (grinning, despite the pain) “C’mon, Leah. I’m fine. Give mine to Avi—his throat’s drier than the Negev.”  
ZH:   - **大卫**：（忍痛咧嘴）“别这样，莉娅。我没事。把我的给阿维——他嗓子干得比内盖夫还厉害。”  

EN:   - **Leah**: (firm) “No heroes. We all need it.” (She tucks the canteen into his pocket.) “Now rest. You’ll need the energy.”  
ZH:   - **莉娅**：（坚定）“别当英雄。我们都需要。”（她把水壶塞回他口袋。）“现在休息。留着力气。”  

EN: * **Subtext**: Leah hates rationing—she became a medic to *help*, not deny. The photo of her niece peeks out from her pocket.  
ZH: * **潜台词**：莉娅厌恶配给——她当医护兵是为了“帮助”，而非拒绝。她口袋里露出侄女的照片。  


EN: ### **Scene 3: The Scout Arrives**  
ZH: ### **场景3：侦察兵抵达**  

EN: * **Action**: Samira slips through the barbed wire, covered in dust. She grabs Dan’s arm, urgent.  
ZH: * **动作**：萨米拉浑身是尘，钻过铁丝网。她急切地抓住丹的胳膊。  

EN: * **Dialogue Notes**:  
ZH: * **对话提示**：  

EN:   - **Samira**: (breathless, in Hebrew) “Lt. Dan! The Syrians—ten tanks, two platoons. They’re hiding in the wadi west of here. Dawn attack—*they think you’re weak*.”  
ZH:   - **萨米拉**：（气喘吁吁，用希伯来语）“丹中尉！叙利亚人——十辆坦克，两个排。藏在西边的干谷里。黎明进攻——*他们觉得你们软弱*。”  

EN:   - **Dan**: (skeptical, but intrigued) “How do you know?”  
ZH:   - **丹**：（怀疑但感兴趣）“你怎么知道的？”  

EN:   - **Samira**: (pulling out a hand-drawn sketch) “My cousin is with their scouts. He told me. (She taps the map.) They’ll come from the olive grove—your left flank is exposed.”  
ZH:   - **萨米拉**：（掏出手绘草图）“我表弟在他们侦察队里。他告诉我的。（轻敲地图）他们会从橄榄园过来——你们的左翼暴露了。”  

EN:   - **Dan**: (pausing, then nodding) “We’ll use your map. Thank you.”  
ZH:   - **丹**：（停顿后点头）“我们用你的地图。谢谢。”  

EN: * **Subtext**: Samira’s voice cracks when she mentions her cousin—she’s risking her family’s safety to help. Dan is wary, but he sees the conviction in her eyes.  
ZH: * **潜台词**：萨米拉提到表弟时声音发颤——她正以家人安全为代价提供帮助。丹仍存戒心，但看到了她眼中的坚定。  


EN: ### **Act 1 Climax**  
ZH: ### **第一幕高潮**  

EN: * **Action**: The tanks rumble louder. Dan stares at Samira’s map, then yells to Moshe: “Get that MG on the left flank! Now!”  
ZH: * **动作**：坦克轰鸣更响。丹盯着萨米拉的地图，冲摩西大喊：“把机枪架到左翼！立刻！”  

EN: * **Tone**: Tension builds—this is not a drill. The unit is outnumbered, but Samira’s intel gives them a fighting chance.  
ZH: * **基调**：紧张升级——这不是演习。部队寡不敌众，但萨米拉的情报让他们有了一线生机。  


EN: ## **Act 2: Escalation**  
ZH: ## **第二幕：升级**  

EN: * **Time**: 4:30 AM, pre-dawn.  
ZH: * **时间**：凌晨4:30，黎明前。  

EN: * **Setting**: The outpost is darker, colder. The tank rumble is *close*—the ground shakes.  
ZH: * **场景**：前哨站更暗、更冷。坦克轰鸣已近在咫尺——地面震动。  


EN: ### **Scene 1: Perimeter (Again)**  
ZH: ### **场景1：外围（再）**  

EN: * **Action**: Dan checks the MG nest—Moshe has fixed it, but only 20 AP rounds remain. The radio crackles.  
ZH: * **动作**：丹检查机枪工事——摩西修好了，但仅剩20发穿甲弹。无线电滋滋作响。  

EN: * **Dialogue Notes**:  
ZH: * **对话提示**：  

EN:   - **Moshe**: (relieved) “Sir, the MG’s good to go. But we’re low on AP—only 20 rounds.”  
ZH:   - **摩西**：（松口气）“长官，机枪修好了。但穿甲弹只剩20发。”  

EN:   - **Dan**: (grim) “Make them count. Aim for the tracks.”  
ZH:   - **丹**：（严峻）“省着用。瞄准履带。”  

EN:   - **Radio**: (static) “Israeli forces, this is HQ. Ceasefire negotiations underway. Stand by for confirmation.”  
ZH:   - **无线电**：（杂音）“以色列部队，这里是指挥部。停火谈判进行中。等待确认。”  

EN:   - **Moshe**: (hopeful) “Sir—does that mean we can stop fighting?”  
ZH:   - **摩西**：（期待）“长官——这意味着我们可以停火了？”  

EN:   - **Dan**: (hesitant) “It means we wait. But keep your finger on the trigger.”  
ZH:   - **丹**：（犹豫）“意味着我们等待。但手指别离开扳机。”  

EN: * **Subtext**: The ceasefire is a false hope—Dan doesn’t trust it, but he needs his men to believe.  
ZH: * **潜台词**：停火是虚假的希望——丹并不相信，但需要部下抱有期待。  


EN: ### **Scene 2: Water Rationing**  
ZH: ### **场景2：配给水**  

EN: * **Action**: Leah hands out canteens to the soldiers. Pvt. Avi begs for more.  
ZH: * **动作**：莉娅给士兵分发水壶。阿维列兵哀求多给些。  

EN: * **Dialogue Notes**:  
ZH: * **对话提示**：  

EN:   - **Avi**: (hoarse) “Leah, c’mon—one more sip. My throat’s on fire.”  
ZH:   - **阿维**：（沙哑）“莉娅，求你了——再喝一口。嗓子快冒烟了。”  

EN:   - **Leah**: (firm, but sad) “Avi, I can’t. If we run out, people die. (She places a hand on his shoulder.) I’m sorry.”  
ZH:   - **莉娅**：（坚定但难过）“阿维，不行。水喝完了，人就会死。（把手搭在他肩上）对不起。”  

EN:   - **Avi**: (angry, but resigned) “Fine. But when I’m dead, tell my mom I died thirsty.”  
ZH:   - **阿维**：（愤怒但无奈）“行吧。但我死了，告诉咱妈我是渴死的。”  

EN: * **Subtext**: Leah’s eyes fill with tears—she’s losing her ability to “fix” things. The canteen in her hand feels like a weapon.  
ZH: * **潜台词**：莉娅眼眶含泪——她逐渐失去“解决问题”的能力。手中的水壶像件凶器。  


EN: ### **Scene 3: Samira’s Update**  
ZH: ### **场景3：萨米拉的情报更新**  

EN: * **Action**: Samira bursts into the command post, her sketch updated. Dan stands, alert.  
ZH: * **动作**：萨米拉冲进指挥所，手中草图已更新。丹警觉起身。  

EN: * **Dialogue Notes**:  
ZH: * **对话提示**：  

EN:   - **Samira**: (panicked) “They moved! The tanks are now behind the olive grove—your left flank is *wide open*.”  
ZH:   - **萨米拉**：（慌乱）“他们动了！坦克现在在橄榄园后方——你们的左翼*完全暴露*了。”  

EN:   - **Dan**: (grabbing the map) “How did you get this?”  
ZH:   - **丹**：（抓过地图）“你怎么弄到的？”  

EN:   - **Samira**: (defiant) “I snuck past their guards. They think Bedouin women are invisible. (She leans in.) Lt. Dan—they’re going to use smoke grenades to hide their advance. You need to set up flares.”  
ZH:   - **萨米拉**：（倔强）“我溜过了他们的岗哨。他们觉得贝都因女人是透明的。（凑近）丹中尉——他们会用烟雾弹掩护推进。你们需要布置照明弹。”  

EN:   - **Avi**: (entering, skeptical) “Why should we trust her? She’s Bedouin—maybe she’s working with them!”  
ZH:   - **阿维**：（走进来，怀疑）“凭什么信她？她是贝都因人——说不定通敌！”  

EN:   - **Dan**: (sharp) “Avi, she risked her life to get us this. Show some respect.”  
ZH:   - **丹**：（严厉）“阿维，她冒着生命危险送情报。放尊重点。”  

EN: * **Subtext**: Samira’s shoulders relax—Dan’s defense of her is the first time anyone in the unit has trusted her. Avi storms out, but the other soldiers watch Samira differently now.  
ZH: * **潜台词**：萨米拉肩头放松——丹的维护是部队首次信任她。阿维摔门而出，但其他士兵看她的目光变了。  


EN: ### **Act 2 Climax**  
ZH: ### **第二幕高潮**  

EN: * **Action**: The radio crackles again—*no confirmation* of the ceasefire. Dan stares at Samira’s map, then says: “Moshe—load the MG with flares. Leah—get everyone to the bunker. Samira—you’re with me. We’re not waiting for HQ.”  
ZH: * **动作**：无线电再次响起——*停火无确认*。丹盯着萨米拉的地图，下令：“摩西——给机枪装照明弹。莉娅——带所有人进掩体。萨米拉——跟我来。不等指挥部了。”  

EN: * **Tone**: Desperation sets in. The ceasefire is a myth—they’re on their own.  
ZH: * **基调**：绝望蔓延。停火是谎言——他们只能靠自己。
