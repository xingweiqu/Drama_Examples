EN: #### **Scene 1: The Clocktower (Dawn)**  
ZH: #### **场景1：钟楼（黎明）**  

EN: *Fog curls around the town’s iconic clocktower, its gears grinding loudly. Javier Lopez, 50s, in a grease-stained jumpsuit, climbs a ladder to the mechanism. He mutters to himself about “the damn clock always acting up.” As he reaches into the gears, his hand hits something soft. He pulls back, gasping, and shines his flashlight—revealing a decomposing arm. He screams, falls back, and scrambles for his phone.  
**Javier (panicked, to 911):** “It’s… it’s a body. In the clocktower. Jesus, it’s been here for months.”*  
ZH: *浓雾笼罩着小镇标志性的钟楼，齿轮发出刺耳的转动声。50多岁的哈维尔·洛佩兹穿着沾有油渍的连体工装，爬上梯子检修机械。他嘟囔着“这破钟总出毛病”。当他把手伸进齿轮时，触到一个柔软的东西。他缩回手倒抽冷气，用手电筒一照——露出一截腐烂的手臂。他尖叫着后退，慌乱摸出手机。

EN: ## **Thematic Notes**  
EN: - **Time as a Weapon:** The clocktower symbolizes how the past (1978 murder) haunts the present (2023 murder). The gears represent the smuggling ring’s “machine”—kept running by silence until it breaks.  
EN: - **Silence vs. Justice:** The play explores how fear (Lua’s hesitation, Javi’s guilt) enables corruption, while speaking up (Lila’s persistence, Javi’s confession) dismantles it.  
EN: - **Family and Legacy:** Lila’s relationship with her father, Javi’s struggle with Carlos, and Tomas/Rico’s desire to protect others highlight how legacy can be both a burden and a catalyst for change.  
EN: 
EN: This structure balances suspense, character depth, and thematic resonance—using the clocktower as a physical and emotional anchor for the story of two murders, one town, and the fight to break the cycle of silence.

ZH: ## **主题注记**  
ZH: - **时间作为武器：** 钟楼象征着过去（1978年的谋杀案）如何纠缠着现在（2023年的谋杀案）。齿轮代表走私集团的“机器”——靠沉默维持运转，直至崩塌。  
ZH: - **沉默与正义：** 故事探讨恐惧（卢娅的犹豫、哈维的愧疚）如何助长腐败，而发声（莉拉的坚持、哈维的坦白）如何瓦解腐败。  
ZH: - **家族与传承：** 莉拉与父亲的关系、哈维与卡洛斯的挣扎，以及托马斯/里科保护他人的愿望，凸显了传承既是负担，也是改变的催化剂。  
ZH: 
ZH: 这一结构平衡了悬疑感、角色深度与主题共鸣——以钟楼作为两起谋杀案、一座小镇及打破沉默循环抗争的物质与情感核心。
