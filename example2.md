EN: # **Play Structure: *Stay***  
ZH: # **戏剧结构：《坚守》**  

EN: A 3-act drama about a working-class family fighting to keep their home amid financial ruin and a greedy landlord’s plans to gentrify their building. The play explores vulnerability, community, and the power of choosing hope when all seems lost.  
ZH: 一部三幕剧，讲述一个工人阶级家庭在经济崩溃和贪婪房东企图将公寓高档化的双重压力下，为保住家园而抗争的故事。该剧探讨了脆弱性、社区联结，以及在一切看似无望时选择希望的力量。

EN: ## **Act Breakdown**  
ZH: ## **幕次梗概**  

EN: ### **Act 1: The Crack in the Foundation**  
ZH: ### **第一幕：根基之裂**  

EN: *Establishes the Carter family’s crisis, their individual struggles, and the looming threat of eviction. Ends with three concurrent failures that deepen their despair.*  
ZH: *铺垫卡特一家的危机、各自的挣扎，以及迫近的驱逐威胁。以三个同时发生的失败事件收尾，加剧绝望。*  


EN: ### **Act 2: The Fallback**  
ZH: ### **第二幕：退而求其次**  

EN: *The family attempts to reverse their misfortune with “plan B” efforts—only to face new obstacles. Jaden uncovers critical evidence about Mr. Voss’s illegal plans, planting a seed of hope.*  
ZH: *全家尝试用“B计划”扭转厄运，却遭遇新阻碍。杰登发现沃斯先生非法计划的关键证据，埋下希望的种子。*  


EN: ### **Act 3: The Stand**  
ZH: ### **第三幕：坚守抗争**  

EN: *Climax in eviction court. The Carters, aided by Mrs. Lopez and the tenant union, challenge Mr. Voss’s illegal actions. The resolution offers a temporary reprieve but emphasizes that their fight is far from over.*  
ZH: *高潮发生在驱逐法庭。卡特一家在洛佩兹太太和租户联盟协助下，挑战沃斯的非法行径。结局暂获喘息，但强调抗争远未结束。*
