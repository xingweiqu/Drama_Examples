EN: ## **Act Breakdown**  
ZH: ## **幕次分解**  

EN: ### **Act 1: The Frozen Truth**  
ZH: ### **第一幕：冻结的真相**  

EN: *Sets up the murder, Maeve’s suspicion of foul play, and her alliance with Jack.*  
ZH: *铺垫谋杀案、梅芙对阴谋的怀疑，以及她与杰克的联盟。*  

EN: #### **Scene 1: The Cannery Freezer**  
ZH: #### **场景1：罐头厂冷藏库**  

EN: **Setting**: Late night. The walk-in freezer of Cole Cannery. Icicles hang from the ceiling; fog curls around metal shelves stacked with fish crates. Blue-white lighting casts harsh shadows.  
ZH: **背景**：深夜。科尔罐头厂的步入式冷藏库。冰锥从天花板垂下；雾气在堆满鱼箱的金属货架间缭绕。蓝白色灯光投下刺眼阴影。  

EN: **Characters**: Jesse, Luis (night crew), Finn (corpse).  
ZH: **角色**：杰西、路易斯（夜班工人），芬恩（尸体）。  

EN: **Action**: Jesse and Luis enter to restock, laughing until they spot Finn’s frozen body slumped against a crate. His throat is slit; a half-empty whiskey bottle lies at his feet. They scream, drop their crates, and flee to call 911.  
ZH: **动作**：杰西和路易斯进入补货，原本有说有笑，直到看到芬恩冻僵的尸体瘫在货箱旁。他的喉咙被割开；脚边有半瓶威士忌。两人尖叫着扔掉货箱，跑出去拨打911。  

EN: **Dialogue Note**: Jesse’s voice cracks as he says, “Luis—*look*. That’s Finn. He’s… he’s not moving.” Luis whispers, “God, no. The boss said he left early. What the hell happened?”  
ZH: **对话提示**：杰西声音发颤地说：“路易斯——*看*。那是芬恩。他……他不动了。”路易斯小声道：“天哪，不。老板说他早走了。到底出了什么事？”  

EN: **Stage Direction**: The camera (or spotlight) lingers on Finn’s hand, which clutches a crumpled union flyer.  
ZH: **舞台指示**：镜头（或聚光灯）停留在芬恩紧攥皱巴巴工会传单的手上。  

EN: #### **Scene 2: The Police Station**  
ZH: #### **场景2：警察局**  

EN: **Setting**: Next morning. A drab police station waiting room. Fluorescent lights buzz. A poster for “Neighborhood Watch” hangs askew.  
ZH: **背景**：次日早晨。单调的警察局等候室。荧光灯嗡嗡作响。一张“社区守望”海报歪斜地挂着。  

EN: **Characters**: Maeve, Detective Lopez.  
ZH: **角色**：梅芙，洛佩兹警探。  

EN: **Action**: Maeve storms in, demanding answers. Lopez informs her Finn’s death is ruled a “drunken accident”—he “stumbled into the freezer and couldn’t get out.” Maeve snaps, “Finn was scared of small spaces since we were kids! He’d never go in there voluntarily!” Lopez dismisses her: “Grief makes people see things, Ms. Flynn. The evidence speaks for itself.”  
ZH: **动作**：梅芙冲进来要求解释。洛佩兹告知她芬恩的死被定性为“醉酒意外”——他“跌进冷藏库后无法出来”。梅芙厉声说：“芬恩从小就怕狭小空间！他绝不会自己进去！”洛佩兹敷衍道：“悲伤会让人产生幻觉，弗林女士。证据已经说明一切。”  

EN: **Dialogue Note**: Maeve slams her fist on the desk: “The evidence? A whiskey bottle? Finn didn’t even drink! You’re not listening!” Lopez sighs, softening: “I’m sorry. But we’ve got no signs of foul play.”  
ZH: **对话提示**：梅芙一拳砸在桌上：“证据？就一个威士忌瓶？芬恩根本不喝酒！你们根本不听！”洛佩兹叹气，语气缓和：“抱歉。但我们没发现他杀迹象。”  

EN: **Key Conflict**: Maeve vs. Lopez (Person vs. Person)—her emotion clashes with his bureaucratic logic.  
ZH: **核心冲突**：梅芙与洛佩兹（人际冲突）——她的情绪与他的官僚逻辑碰撞。  

EN: #### **Scene 3: Finn’s Apartment**  
ZH: #### **场景3：芬恩的公寓**  

EN: **Setting**: Afternoon. A cramped, cluttered apartment in a working-class neighborhood. Union posters (“Fight for Fair Wages!”) cover the walls. A mug with “Union Strong” sits on the kitchen table.  
ZH: **背景**：午后。工人社区一间狭小杂乱的公寓。墙上贴满工会海报（“为公平工资而战！”）。厨房桌上摆着刻有“工会强韧”的马克杯。  

EN: **Characters**: Maeve, Jack (off-stage voice).  
ZH: **角色**：梅芙，杰克（画外音）。  

EN: **Action**: Maeve sifts through Finn’s things, crying as she finds his childhood stuffed bear (a gift from her). She spots a locked desk drawer; using a hairpin (Finn taught her how), she opens it to find his union journal. As she reads entries about “Walter Cole’s land grab” and threats (“Stay out of it or you’ll regret it”), Jack knocks. He introduces himself as a reporter who knew Finn—“He helped my sister get her job back. I owe him.” Maeve hesitates, then hands him the journal. “Help me prove he was murdered.”  
ZH: **动作**：梅芙翻找芬恩的物品，发现他童年的毛绒熊（她送的礼物）时哭了。她注意到锁着的书桌抽屉，用发夹（芬恩教过她）打开，找到他的工会日记。她读到“沃尔特·科尔的土地掠夺”和威胁（“别插手，否则你会后悔”）的条目时，杰克敲门。他自称是认识芬恩的记者——“他帮我妹妹找回了工作，我欠他的。”梅芙犹豫后递过日记：“帮我证明他是被谋杀的。”  

EN: **Dialogue Note**: Jack flips through the journal: “Jesus. He was onto something big. Cole’s been trying to push out the waterfront neighborhoods for years.” Maeve: “Then let’s take him down. For Finn.”  
ZH: **对话提示**：杰克翻着日记：“天哪。他发现了大秘密。科尔多年来一直想赶走滨水社区的居民。”梅芙：“那我们就扳倒他。为了芬恩。”  

EN: **Causal Link**: Finn’s journal (3.3) triggers their investigation into the land deal (3.4).  
ZH: **因果关联**：芬恩的日记（3.3）触发了他们对土地交易的调查（3.4）。

EN: ### **Act 2: Uncovering the Web**  
ZH: ### **第二幕：揭开罗网**  

EN: *The investigation escalates: Maeve and Jack dig into the land deal, interview a scared witness, and get forensic proof of tampering.*  
ZH: *调查升级：梅芙与杰克深挖土地交易，约谈惊恐的目击者，并获得锁具被破坏的法医证据。*  

EN: #### **Scene 1: City Hall Records Room**  
ZH: #### **场景1：市政厅档案室**  

EN: **Setting**: Midday. A dusty, windowless room stacked with file cabinets. A clock ticks loudly.  
ZH: **背景**：正午。满是灰尘的无窗房间，堆满文件柜。钟表滴答作响。  

EN: **Characters**: Maeve, Jack, Mayor Hale (off-stage).  
ZH: **角色**：梅芙，杰克，黑尔市长（画外音）。  

EN: **Action**: Maeve and Jack sift through land records, discovering that Cole bought 10 acres of waterfront property—*including the low-income housing complex where Finn’s union members live*. Jack finds a memo: “Mayor Hale approves rezoning—$50k donation to campaign.” Maeve snarls: “They’re gonna bulldoze people’s homes for condos. Finn was stopping them.” Jack: “And Cole killed him for it.”  
ZH: **动作**：梅芙和杰克查阅土地档案，发现科尔买下了10英亩滨水土地——*包括芬恩工会成员居住的低收入公寓楼*。杰克找到一份备忘录：“黑尔市长批准重新划区——竞选资金获捐5万美元。”梅芙怒道：“他们要推平民宅建公寓。芬恩在阻止他们。”杰克：“所以科尔杀了他。”  

EN: **Dialogue Note**: Jack slams the memo on the table: “Hale’s been in Cole’s pocket for years. This is bigger than just a murder.” Maeve: “Good. Then we’ll take down both of them.”  
ZH: **对话提示**：杰克将备忘录拍在桌上：“黑尔早就是科尔的傀儡了。这案子不止是谋杀。”梅芙：“正好。那我们就一起扳倒他们。”  

EN: #### **Scene 2: Maria’s Apartment**  
ZH: #### **场景2：玛丽亚的公寓**  

EN: **Setting**: Evening. A tiny, warm apartment in the waterfront complex. A baby cries in the next room.  
ZH: **背景**：傍晚。滨水社区一间狭小温馨的公寓。隔壁房间传来婴儿啼哭。  

EN: **Characters**: Maeve, Jack, Maria.  
ZH: **角色**：梅芙，杰克，玛丽亚。  

EN: **Action**: Maria is jumpy, picking at her nails. She admits she saw Finn arguing with a “masked guy” in the cannery parking lot the night he died. “The guy grabbed his throat—said ‘Stay away from the land deal.’ Then Finn pushed him off and ran. I… I didn’t call the police. I was scared.” Just as she hands Maeve a photo of the masked figure (blurry, but wearing a Cole Cannery jacket), her phone buzzes. She gasps: “It’s a text. ‘Keep your mouth shut or your kid gets hurt.’” She shoves the photo at Maeve and runs to the bedroom, locking the door.  
ZH: **动作**：玛丽亚焦躁不安，不停啃指甲。她承认芬恩死亡当晚在罐头厂停车场看到他与“戴面具的人”争执：“那人掐他脖子——说‘别插手土地交易’。然后芬恩推开他跑了。我……我没报警，我害怕。”她刚把面具人的照片（模糊，但穿着科尔罐头厂夹克）递给梅芙，手机就响了。她倒吸一口气：“是短信。‘闭上嘴，否则你孩子遭殃。’”她把照片塞给梅芙，冲进卧室锁上门。  

EN: **Dialogue Note**: Maria whispers, “I’m sorry. I can’t lose my baby.” Maeve: “We’ll protect you. Just tell us the truth.” Jack: “That jacket—Cole’s maintenance crew wears those. The masked guy was one of his men.”  
ZH: **对话提示**：玛丽亚小声说：“对不起。我不能失去孩子。”梅芙：“我们会保护你。只要说出真相。”杰克：“那件夹克——科尔的维修工人都穿这种。面具人是他的手下。”  

EN: **Causal Link**: Maria’s testimony (3.5) leads them to the forensic lab (3.6).  
ZH: **因果关联**：玛丽亚的证词（3.5）引导他们前往法医实验室（3.6）。  

EN: #### **Scene 3: Forensic Lab**  
ZH: #### **场景3：法医实验室**  

EN: **Setting**: Late night. A sterile, white lab with microscopes and tool benches. Dr. Mendez stands over a table with the freezer lock.  
ZH: **背景**：深夜。整洁的白色实验室，摆满显微镜和工具台。门德斯博士站在桌前，桌上放着冷藏库锁具。  

EN: **Characters**: Maeve, Jack, Dr. Mendez.  
ZH: **角色**：梅芙，杰克，门德斯博士。

EN: **Action**: Dr. Mendez holds up a metal tool: “This is a hex wrench from Cole’s maintenance shed. The scratches on the lock match—someone tampered with it. The door was *locked from the outside*.” Maeve’s eyes fill with tears: “So he was forced in. They left him to freeze to death.” Jack: “And the whiskey bottle? Staged.” Dr. Mendez: “The toxicology report said his blood alcohol was .02—barely a sip. The bottle was planted.” Maeve slams her fist on the table: “We’ve got him.”  
ZH: **行动**: 门德斯医生举起一件金属工具：“这是从科尔维修棚里找到的六角扳手。锁上的划痕吻合——有人动过手脚。门是*从外面锁上的*。”梅芙眼眶含泪：“所以他是被强行关进去的。他们留他在里面冻死。”杰克：“那威士忌酒瓶呢？是伪造的？”门德斯医生：“毒理学报告显示他血液酒精含量0.02——几乎只喝了一口。酒瓶是故意放置的。”梅芙一拳砸在桌上：“我们抓住他了。”  

EN: **Dialogue Note**: Dr. Mendez: “I don’t like covering up murders, Detective… but Lopez didn’t want to hear it. Until you showed up.” Maeve: “Thank you. For telling the truth.”  
ZH: **对话备注**: 门德斯医生：“我不喜欢掩盖谋杀案，警探……但洛佩兹根本不听。直到你出现。”梅芙：“谢谢你。说出真相。”  

EN: **Key Escalation**: Forensic proof (3.6) gives them the leverage to confront Cole (3.7).  
ZH: **关键升级**: 法医证据（3.6）为他们提供了与科尔对峙的筹码（3.7）。

EN: ### **Act 3: The Confrontation**  
EN: *Maeve and Jack confront Walter Cole; he confesses, and justice is served.*  
ZH: ### **第三幕：对峙**

EN: #### **Scene 1: Walter Cole’s Office**  
EN: **Setting**: Morning. A sleek, modern office with floor-to-ceiling windows overlooking the cannery. A desk made of reclaimed wood (a “rustic” touch for PR) holds a photo of Cole with Mayor Hale.  
EN: **Characters**: Maeve, Jack, Walter Cole, Detective Lopez (off-stage).  
EN: **Action**: Cole is calm, pouring himself a scotch, when Maeve and Jack burst in. Maeve slams the forensic report on his desk: “We know you tampered with the freezer lock. We know you threatened Finn. Confess.” Cole laughs: “You have no proof. The police ruled it an accident.” Jack pulls out the memo from City Hall: “We have proof you bribed the mayor. We have Maria’s testimony. We have the jacket from your maintenance crew.” Cole’s smile fades. “Finn was a pest. He was gonna stop the land deal—stop *me*. So I had my guy take care of him. Locked him in the freezer. Planted the whiskey. Easy.” Maeve steps closer: “Why? Because you’re greedy? Because you don’t care about the people who work for you?” Cole sneers: “The people? They’re replaceable. I’m not.” Just then, Lopez bursts in, holding a warrant: “Walter Cole, you’re under arrest for the murder of Finn Flynn.”  
EN: **Dialogue Note**: Cole: “You think this will stop me? The land deal’s already approved!” Maeve: “Not anymore. We leaked the memo to the press. The mayor’s resigning. Your empire’s crumbling.” Cole glares at her: “You’ll regret this, Flynn.” Maeve: “No. *You* will.”  
EN: **Climax**: Cole’s confession (3.7) resolves the central conflict (Maeve vs. Cole).  
ZH: #### **场景1：沃尔特·科尔的办公室**  
ZH: **场景设定**：清晨。一间线条流畅的现代办公室，落地窗俯瞰着罐头厂。一张再生木办公桌（为公关打造的“质朴”风格）上摆着科尔与黑尔市长的合影。  
ZH: **角色**：梅芙、杰克、沃尔特·科尔、洛佩兹警探（幕后）。  
ZH: **行动**：科尔正从容倒着苏格兰威士忌，梅芙与杰克破门而入。梅芙将法医报告拍在桌上：“我们知道你篡改了冷冻库门锁。我们知道你威胁过芬恩。招了吧。”科尔笑道：“你们没证据。警方已判定是意外。”杰克抽出市政厅备忘录：“我们有你贿赂市长的证据。我们有玛丽亚的证词。我们有你维修队的外套。”科尔笑容褪去：“芬恩是个麻烦。他要阻止土地交易——阻止*我*。所以我让手下解决他。锁进冷冻库，放了威士忌酒瓶。简单。”梅芙逼近：“为什么？因为贪婪？因为你不在乎为你工作的人？”科尔冷笑：“那些人？可以替代。我不行。”此时洛佩兹举着逮捕令冲进来：“沃尔特·科尔，你因谋杀芬恩·弗林被捕。”  
ZH: **对话备注**: 科尔：“你以为这能阻止我？土地交易已经获批！”梅芙：“现在不了。我们把备忘录泄露给了媒体。市长要辞职。你的帝国要垮了。”科尔瞪着她：“你会后悔的，弗林。”梅芙：“不。*你*才会。”  
ZH: **高潮**：科尔的供认（3.7）解决了核心冲突（梅芙与科尔的对抗）。  

EN: #### **Scene 2: Finn’s Grave**  
EN: **Setting**: Afternoon. A small cemetery overlooking the waterfront. Daffodils (Finn’s favorite) blow in the wind.  
EN: **Characters**: Maeve, Jack, Detective Lopez, Cannery Workers (ensemble).  
EN: **Action**: Maeve stands at Finn’s grave, holding his union journal. Jack and Lopez join her. Lopez: “The DA’s pressing charges. Cole’s going to prison.” Maeve nods: “Good.” Jack: “The union’s asked me to write a series about Finn.
